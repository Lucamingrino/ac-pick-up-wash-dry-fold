Atlantic City Laundry FAQ

IMPORTANT: Leaving Laundry at Front Desk/Valet

DO NOT leave your laundry with the bell hop, valet, or front desk unless explicitly pre-arranged with AC Wash Dry Fold Dispatch and the specific hotel attendant.

The hotel is a separate entity and is not responsible for your belongings. To ensure the safety of your items, we strongly recommend meeting our driver directly. If a handoff is absolutely necessary, you must confirm the specific attendant's name with our dispatch first.

Do you provide laundry pickup at casinos?

Yes, we specialize in casino laundry services. We can meet you at the valet or main entrance of any major Atlantic City resort, including Borgata, Hard Rock, Ocean, Tropicana, Harrah's, and Caesars.

How fast is your wash and fold service?

We offer Super Rush (4-hour turnaround), Rush (Same Day delivery by 8 PM), and Standard Next Day delivery. We are the fastest pick up & delivery laundry service in Atlantic City.

Do you service Airbnbs and Vacation Rentals?

Absolutely. We provide linen and laundry turnover services for Airbnbs and vacation rentals throughout Atlantic City, Ventnor, and Margate.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and Apple Pay. Payment is easily processed via a secure link sent to your phone once your order is weighed and processed.
